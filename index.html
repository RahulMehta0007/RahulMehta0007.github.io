About Us

Pinnaculo is a leading Business & Training provider helping Students & professionals across industries and sectors to develop new expertise through online and offline trainings and bridge their skill gaps for recognition and growth in the corporate world. Evolved and Developed with the intention of delivering high value training through innovative and practical approaches, Pinnaculo offers a wide range of services in training, learning and development in the field of Emerging technology and Management. The founders of the company are young entrepreneurs, who were motivated by the need to fill a niche in the IT Training industry for professionals and they are aided in their goal by industry experts who conduct the Trainings igniting minds and motivating Students & professionals to face on-the-job challenges.

Our Mission

We aspire to make Pinnacluo. A leading market leader for its learning platform and one stop solution for consulting, recognized for its professional Learning & development and academic programs that prepare students for the challenges through our research & discovery to make them industry masters in their fields.

Our Plan

Our corporate trainings have been designed with the experts keeping the technology in mind and we offer you customized solutions for all your training needs. Pinnaculo is an extended solutions partner to organizations seeking consulting or training services to enhance workforce performance and drive business success.

Our Vision

To provide quality and cost efficient academic and professional training designed to meet the unique requirements of professionals across the globe. Delivery of quality education in a variety of flexible settings to meet the diverse needs of professionals. Our work is guided by our value, openness, and integrity.
